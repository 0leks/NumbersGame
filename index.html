Numbers Game

Rules

To start the game, each player chooses a secret 4 digit number. Duplicate digits are not allowed. Then, players take turns guessing the other person's secret number. For each guess, players give a hint as to the accuracy of the guess. The hint consists of two numbers. The first is the number of digits correct and the second is number of those digits in the correct place.

For example, if the secret number is 5678 and the guess is 8627, then the hint is 3 1.
  • The first number is 3 because 6, 7, and 8 are all digits of the secret number.
  • The second number is 1 because 6 is in the correct place in the guess. (Second in both guess and secret number)

Example Game

Player A secret number: 0123
Player B secret number: 5387
Round 1
A guesses 0123, B gives hint 1 0
B guesses 9256, A gives hint 1 0
Round 2
A guesses 4567, B gives hint 2 1
B guesses 0134, A gives hint 3 2
Round 3
A guesses 9452, B gives hint 1 0
B guesses 4135, A gives hint 2 1
Round 4
A guesses 3768, B gives hint 3 0
B guesses 9034, A gives hint 2 0
Round 5
A guesses 6397, B gives hint 2 2
B guesses 0123, which is the correct number!
